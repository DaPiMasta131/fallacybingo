Fallacy Bingo Generator

Hello user! this is a program to generate Fallacy Bingo cards, they are at quite high resolution at default, so don't generate too many!

amount is the amount of sheets to generate (there are four cards on each sheet so the number of cards is four times the amount you get), size is the resolution of the cards (width), GENERATE will generate however many sheets you requested and place them on screen (be careful, this can crash the page with too many at a high resolution!)

when printing out the cards, set margins to None, it is designed so that pennies fit perfectly on the squares as tokens

To save as a PDF: press ctrl+P after you have generated the desired set of sheets and set "Margins" to "None"
